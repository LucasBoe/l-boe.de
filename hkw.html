About:

This was a cooperation project with the HKW, one of the most important cultural institutions in berlin.

Click here to visit their website.

The goal was to analyze their workflows and progress in an artistic way. With a small team of four, we used the media of games to attend the ‘Introspection’.

During the process we created four prototypes, a card game and polished a final game, as well as prepared the exhibition, processed our material and constructed an arcade Machine to present our final game in the best way possible.

My Part:

  • Game Design
  • Prototyping
  • Exhibition preparation
  • Workflow adaptation
  • Interviews
  • Arcade Machine construction
