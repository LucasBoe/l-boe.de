About:

Dracula Inn is a visual prototype, a modern interpretation of the classical dracula material.

You travel to Draculas Castle as a detective / journalist / businessman. In all the roles you have diffenrent interests and abilitys to discover the truth behind the rumors about dracula.

My Part:

  • Narrative Design

All the Artworks have a different aproach in the used color sceme, number of colors, and all try to convay a different feeling and situation trough their appereance.
