I'm a Game Designer & creator of interactive Adventures and Films, based in Berlin.

I make games as a hobby since I have been able to talk.
I love to work on interesting ideas and develop them into compelling prototypes that people enjoy playing.
Games are my favourite medium because they are able to evoke emotions through their interactivity like no other.

Core Skills:

  • - Game Design
  • - Prototyping
  • - Teammanagement
In order to produce compelling game experiences and solid prototypes I can draw from a broad skillset of game development skills such as:
  • - programming (Unity C#)
  • - 3D Asset creation(Maya, Substance Painter)
  • - and 2D Graphic Design/Asset Creation (Photoshop)

I’m interested in history, architecture, in playing the guitar, and I like cycling in order to enjoy the beauty of nature and to escape the fast-moving city of berlin.

Also I'm a Dungeon Master for Dungeons & Dragons and use my spare time to write campaigns, short stories or plays. Once or twice a year I get together with friends in order to make a new movie or short film.

I really enjoy spending my evening reading fantasy, science fiction, historical novels or classic literature.
